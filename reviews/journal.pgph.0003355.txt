5. Review Comments to the Author
Reviewer #1: The work has the potential to contribute to the field of HIV and FSW thus relevant. Detailed comments and recommendations are given in the attached document to increase the quality of the paper to the standard that is acceptable measured by ease of understanding by the reader.
Reviewer #2: Review “You know that we travel a lot”: Mobility narratives among female sex workers living with HIV in Tanzania and the Dominican Republic”
This qualitative study describes the mobility narratives among female sex workers in two settings, Tanzania and Dominican Republic.
The study is highly informative in gaining further understanding into the motivations and patterns of mobility of FSWs, which further informs FSW typology. This is crucial, as previous quantitative studies were not sufficiently able to provide accurate typology of FSW mobility patterns.
The paper is well-written and the analysis is thorough. I recommend publication.
